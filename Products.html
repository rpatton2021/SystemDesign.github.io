Welcome to Harry and Harriet's Hang Gliding Haven

Our Products

  1. Hang Gliding Kit

    Our hang-gliding kit comes with the safest and newest of gear (listed under our gear section). We have two options: one with a professional or the solo version. Our kit is for an hour of flying time with a 30 minute lesson on how to operate the gear.

    What's Included:

    • Hang glider
    • Harness
    • Helmet
    • Instruction manual

  2. Hang Gliding Gear

    Our hang-gliding gear is the safest and newest technology. We make sure our gear follows the safety measures described under the safe sports PDF and make sure the quality is up to standards. We test out our equipment once a month to give the safest experience for our customers.

    What's Included:

    • Hang gliding suit
    • Foot launch system
    • Wind gauge
    • Emergency parachute

  3. Junior Hang Gliding Kit

    This is our kit for those who are newer to hang-gliding or skydiving. This lasts about an hour (similar to our main hang-gliding package). We make sure to take extra precautions and require a professional to fly with those who use this kit.

    What's Included:

    • Mini hang glider
    • Junior harness
    • Junior helmet
    • Beginner's guide
